Software usability case study

Dec 4, 2014 • 7:54 PM

My daughter is required to use Rosetta Stone on her (school-mandated) iPad for 7th grade Spanish. Recently, it stopped letting her log in, showing the helpful message "ML390: LCP: Session expired":

The usual iPad reboot, app uninstall/reinstall, consulting with school staff, etc. did not fix it.

I eventually found the problem: the date on her iPad was set wrong - fairly obvious in retrospect.

The error message was not helpful.
